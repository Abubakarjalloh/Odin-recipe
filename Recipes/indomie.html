Indomie

Description

The dish is finished with crisp garnishes that pop

like little accents of texture and flair, giving it a touch of

culinary confidence. On the side, delicately crisped toppings lean casually against the noodles, almost like edible flames

,

inviting you to mix, match, and explore every bite. Resting atop a textured mat with

warm yellow surroundings, the whole setting feels like an invitation to comfort — the kind of comfort that only a plate of Indomie,

plated with pride and perfection, can deliver. This isn’t just a meal; it’s a story told in color, shape, and delicious simplicity.

Ingredients

Noodles
Seasoning powder
Chili powder
Onion
Green peas
Carrots
Red pepper
Tomato
Dried meat or suya
Parsley

Boil water in a pot over medium heat.
Add the Indomie noodles and let them cook for 2-3 minutes.
Stir gently to loosen the strands and prevent sticking.
Add the seasoning powder, chili powder, and oil from the sachets.
Mix well until the noodles absorb the flavors.
Toss in chopped onions, carrots, green peas, and sliced peppers if desired.
Let it simmer for another minute until the veggies are slightly tender.
Turn off the heat and serve hot.
Garnish with dried meat (suya) and fresh parsley for extra flavor and presentation.
